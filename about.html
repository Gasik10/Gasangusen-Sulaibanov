About Me

I am a PhD candidate at École Pratique des Hautes Études (EPHE), PSL, Paris, working on the documentation and description of Tsugni Dargwa, a Caucasian language of Dagestan, Russia.

My research interests include language description and documentation, Nakh-Daghestanian languages, valency, morphology, linguistic typology, language contact and areal linguistics, ideophones, grammaticalization, attributivisation, morphosyntax, cognitive linguistics, and linguistic fieldwork.

I hold an M.A. in Linguistics from EPHE (2021) and a B.A. in Law from Makhachkala State Technical University (2018). I have attended international schools on language documentation and linguistics in France, Italy, Netherlands, and Turkey. I am fluent in Russian, Tsugni Dargwa, French, English, and Italian, with upper-intermediate proficiency in Turkish and elementary knowledge of Arabic.
