About Me

I am a PhD candidate at École Pratique des Hautes Études (EPHE), PSL, Paris, working on the documentation and description of Tsugni Dargwa, a Caucasian language of Dagestan, Russia. My research centers on syntax, ideophones, adverbs, and grammaticalization, drawing on data from fieldwork.

I hold an M.A. in Linguistics from EPHE (2021) and have attended international schools on language documentation. I am fluent in Russian, Tsugni Dargwa, French, English, and Italian, with intermediate proficiency in Turkish and basic knowledge of Arabic.
